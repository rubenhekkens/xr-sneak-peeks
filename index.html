XR Sneak Peeks

Five demo directions distilled from your projects — jarvis, digitaltwin, explodeddrillassembly, chipvisualizer, powersoften and musicvisualizer. Each is a single self-contained HTML file. v2 — cinematic pass: ACES film grading, bloom, grain & chromatic aberration on every demo, plus a running machine, beat detection, holographic glitches and more. Try them all, pick one to take further.

SNEAK PEEK 01

Ghost Flow

A live, interactive gas-purge curtain: a contaminant plume attacks an optic and you command the jet that protects it — protection efficiency computed in real time. Full version: your Noether surrogate running live behind the sliders, in VR.

Teardown

Your real drill assembly — the 14-part Tripo FBX from your Unity project with its full PBR texture sets — loaded live, exploding and reassembling under HDRI lighting. Full version: grab parts with Quest hand tracking.

Silicon Dive

Powers of Ten, but into a chip: one continuous scroll from motherboard to package, die, interconnect, FinFET, and finally the silicon lattice itself — ten orders of magnitude. Full version: the dive happens around you in VR, narrated.

SNEAK PEEK 04

Terrainwave

Your music carved into a mountain range you fly through — drop in an MP3 (Tame Impala works) or use the built-in synth track. Full version: a VR world whose geometry, weather and colour are the song.

Holo Twin

A Jarvis-style holographic control room for a vacuum vessel: live telemetry, sparklines, an event log — and an anomaly you can inject and watch propagate through the hologram. Full version: driven by real sensor data, standing on your desk in AR.
